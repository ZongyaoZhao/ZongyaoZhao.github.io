I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at University of Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Yuanwei Liu. I received the Ph.D. degree from Tsinghua university in 2025, M.Eng degree from Tsinghua university in 2021, and B.Eng. degree from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China in 2018.

My research focuses on integrated sensing and communications (ISAC), statistical signal processing, machine learning, and optimization theory for next-generation wireless systems.
